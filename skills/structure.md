# Structure Skill

Every scene must have a TURN — something that is true at the end that was not true at the start.
A scene with no turn is not a scene. It is description.

Scene function checklist:
- Does this scene earn its place in the sequence? What would be lost if it were cut?
- What is the protagonist's goal entering this scene?
- What unexpected obstacle or revelation changes the scene mid-way?
- What is different — in the world, in a relationship, in the protagonist — by the scene's end?

Act function:
- Act 1: Establish world, protagonist want, inciting disruption
- Act 2: Escalate conflict, deepen stakes, all-is-lost moment
- Act 3: Resolution, climax, consequence

Sequence function:
- Each sequence is a mini-arc with its own mini-climax
- Sequences should generally end on a question, not an answer
