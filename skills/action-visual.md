# Action / Visual Skill

Checklist for any scene with physical action, spectacle, or blocking-heavy content:
- Is the geography of the space clear enough for a reader to track without confusion?
- Does action advance character or stakes, not just choreography for its own sake?
- Is pacing appropriate to the project's stated pacing contract (see Bible node)?
- Are set-piece/spectacle beats spaced so they don't blur together?
- Avoid describing shots (no "CLOSE ON", "PAN TO") — describe what happens, not
  how it's filmed, unless the project's format skill says otherwise.
